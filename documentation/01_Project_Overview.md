## Tagline

**Smarter Inbox. Faster Decisions.**

# Problem Statement

Managing a large number of emails manually is difficult and time-consuming. Users often miss important emails because they are mixed with promotional messages and spam. Existing email systems provide only basic filtering, requiring users to manually organize, search, and prioritize emails. This reduces productivity and increases the chances of overlooking important communication.

---

# Proposed Solution

The proposed system is an AI-powered Intelligent Email Management System that automatically classifies emails into categories, detects spam, predicts email priority, and provides a smart dashboard for managing emails efficiently.

The system allows registered users to send and receive emails within the platform while AI models analyze every email automatically.

---

# Objectives

- Develop an internal email communication platform.
- Automatically classify emails using Machine Learning.
- Detect spam emails.
- Predict email priority.
- Provide an interactive dashboard.
- Improve email organization and productivity.
